Routes to India

India has been accessible via various routes throughout history:

  • Silk Road
  • Maritime routes via the Indian Ocean
  • Land routes through the Khyber Pass
  • Modern air routes

In recent times, India has developed an extensive network of highways, railways, and ports to enhance connectivity.

Favorable Geographical Features

India's geographical features contribute to its diverse climate and rich resources:

  • Extensive coastline with major ports
  • Fertile river plains like the Ganges
  • Diverse ecosystems from Himalayas to Thar Desert
  • Abundant natural resources

India's varied topography supports a wide range of flora and fauna, making it a biodiversity hotspot.
